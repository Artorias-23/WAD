PICT B.E. Course Structure

Course Structure for B.E.

First Year (FE)

  • Mathematics I: Basics of calculus and linear algebra.
  • Physics I: Fundamentals of mechanics, optics, and waves.
  • C Programming: Introduction to C programming and algorithms.
  • Engineering Drawing: Techniques of sketching and drawing engineering designs.
  • Basic Electrical Engineering: Introduction to electrical circuits and systems.

Second Year (SE)

  • Mathematics II: Advanced calculus and differential equations.
  • Physics II: Study of electromagnetism and thermodynamics.
  • Digital Logic Design: Design and analysis of digital systems.
  • Object-Oriented Programming (OOP): Introduction to OOP concepts using C++.
  • Data Structures: Study of various data structures and algorithms.

Third Year (TE)

  • Discrete Mathematics: Study of mathematical structures used in computing.
  • Operating Systems: Understanding of operating system concepts and components.
  • Database Management Systems: Concepts of data storage, retrieval, and manipulation.
  • Computer Networks: Study of computer communication and networking protocols.
  • Software Engineering: Principles and practices of software development.

Final Year (BE)

  • Advanced Algorithms: Deep dive into advanced algorithmic techniques.
  • Web Technologies: Study of web development frameworks and design.
  • Mobile Computing: Introduction to mobile computing and application development.
  • Project Management: Principles of managing a project lifecycle effectively.
  • Elective - Machine Learning: Introduction to machine learning algorithms and techniques.
